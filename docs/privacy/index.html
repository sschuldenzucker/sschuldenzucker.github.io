Privacy Information

Unless you subscribe to my email newsletter, I do not collect any personal data. This site does not use any cookies.

This website is hosted on Github via GitHub Pages. GitHub collects some technical information to maintain security and integrity. For details see GitHub's privacy policy and their global privacy practices.

When you click “Subscribe” at the top or the TinyLetter icon at the bottom of the page, you are redirected to the service TinyLetter, which I use to manage my newsletter. You can review TinyLetter's privacy policy before you subscribe. If you do, I will have access to your email address. I will also have statistical information about which of my emails you opened and how many links you followed. I will only use your email address for the purpose stated on the TinyLetter subscription page. I will only use the statistical information to measure and improve the performance of my blog and to understand people's interests. I will not share any of this information with anyone else, except for my legitimate interest with respect to the above purposes (such as storing the data in a cloud storage service, sending emails using an email provider, statistical analysis using an app, etc.). I will do my best to store your information securely within the scope of regular computing work.

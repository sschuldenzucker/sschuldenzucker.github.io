Hi, I'm Steffen!

I write about the interactions between technology, society, and consciousness. — Having said that, this includes the piece of consciousness inside my own skull, so pretty much anything goes.

Posts are experimental “idea sketches” more than my so-called “opinion.” Epistemic status is at least contested, often fictional, usually indicated. Views are often not original, sometimes to the point of fan fiction. I try to indicate influences, but sometimes I forget. That's just how it is.

I also do some more serious work on financial markets and algorithmic game theory. I have a CS PhD and a math MSc. My CV is here.

I live in Frankfurt in Germany.
